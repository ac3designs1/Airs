FPO Caution
Hello! My name is (YOUR NAME) from Melbourne police. A firearms prohibition order (FPO) of (Tier type) is going to be placed on you for (time period). An FPO (Firearms prohibition order) prohibits you from acquiring, possessing or carrying a firearm or firearm related items. During the time of you holding an FPO police officers have the right to search you, your vehicle and other individuals you have in your vehicle at any point in time. If you violate your FPO you can receive a higher tier FPO and all your vehicles and premises can be searched. Do you understand this FPO being placed onto you?

Lawyers
If a suspect wants a lawyer to represent them, ensure that the lawyer has the correct paperwork, to check this get them to show you their BAR-LICENSE! If they have no ID or they aren't willing to give it then do not let them in the holding area. If they are a registered lawyer make sure they have not been charged with anything in the last 30 days. If they have, do not let them in! Before letting a lawyer in, make sure you search the lawyer and all bags they have to ensure they are not carrying any illegal weapons, drugs, or materials.
Searching
MAKE SURE YOU READ OUT THE SUSPECT CAUTION BEFORE SEARCHING THEM!!
This does not apply to detained People of Interest after Code 15's or searching lawyers before they represent a suspect
